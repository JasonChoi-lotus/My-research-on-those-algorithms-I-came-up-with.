# Gotha_Windsor Protocol

## Etymology and Core Concept: The Gotha-Windsor Framework
(alternatively designated as the G-W Protocol) derives its nomenclature from the historical wartime transmutation of 
the British Royal Family’s surname from Saxe-Coburg and Gotha to Windsor during the First World War (Identity Transmutation).



---



## Operational Purpose 
Mirroring this historical precedent, 
the G-W Protocol is a highly specialized analytical framework engineered to track, quantify, and model 
the trajectories of socio-linguistic assimilation among minority populations within a dominant host society, alongside adjacent geopolitical and socio-economic dynamics.


This program is planned to be embodied by using `Python matplotlib` as a backend and `HTML` as a frontend.
